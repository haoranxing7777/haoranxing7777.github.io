Mars (4th)

Elon Musk envisions making humanity a multi planetary species

Through his company SpaceX, he aims to establish a sustainable human presence on Mars by developing the Starship rocket, which could transport people and resources to the Red Planet. Musk has even talked about the long-term goal of creating a city on Mars, where people could live and thrive—definitely a bold vision for our future!
