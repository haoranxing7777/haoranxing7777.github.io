Jupiter (5th)

"A giant in the solar system"

Strong magnetic field

Which is about 20,000 times stronger than Earth's! This powerful magnetic field creates intense radiation belts around the planet, making it a challenging environment for spacecraft. Additionally, Jupiter's magnetic field is generated by its rapid rotation—Jupiter spins on its axis once about every 10 hours, which is quite fast considering its size!
