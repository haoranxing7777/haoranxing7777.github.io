Neptune (8th)

"A distant ice giant."

"Neptune: The Windy Ice Giant"

Neptune has the strongest winds in the solar system, with speeds reaching up to 2,100 kilometers per hour (about 1,300 miles per hour). These fierce winds create massive storms and dynamic weather patterns, including the planet's famous Great Dark Spot, which is similar to Jupiter's Great Red Spot.
