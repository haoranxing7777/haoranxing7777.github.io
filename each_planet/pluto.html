"Inability to Clear Its Orbit."

When Pluto Gets Closer to the Sun Than Neptune

Pluto is usually farther from the Sun than Neptune, but due to its elliptical orbit, Pluto sometimes moves closer to the Sun and Earth than Neptune does. This last happened between 1979 and 1999 and won't happen again until 2227. However, they'll never collide because their orbits are in sync!
