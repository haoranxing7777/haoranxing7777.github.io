Mercury (1st)

"Extreme heat, Extreme cold."

Mercury's thin atmosphere.

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and experiences extreme temperature changes. During the day, temperatures can reach up to 800°F (427°C), while at night, they can drop to -330°F (-201°C). This occurs because Mercury has a very thin atmosphere that cannot retain heat.
